About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote sustainability and combat the increasing problem of garbage pollution. We believe that the use of technology, specifically machine learning and advanced algorithms, can help us achieve this goal.

Our Approach

We aim to detect and address the problem of trash pollution by using advanced algorithms to detect trash in images and videos. This information can then be used by local governments, activists, and communities to clean up their surroundings more efficiently.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to finding innovative solutions to the problem of garbage pollution and strive to make a positive impact on the environment.
