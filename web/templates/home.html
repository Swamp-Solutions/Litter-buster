Dedication to sustainability

For most of the history of mankind, we were unable to have any vast impact on the enviroment, however this all changed rapidly in the footsteps of industrialization.

As we learn more about our impact on the climate, the more apparent the need for action has become. While many focus on mitigating and minimizing emissions of green house gases, we decided to combat the ever increasing garbage pollution.

As materialistic demands increase, so does our accumulation of garbage, which of the most ends up in the ocean or other bodies of water. Plastics turn into micro-plastics, which in turn turns into nano-plastics, and today, there are close to no places left without traces of human pollution.

Therefore we introduce ways to leverage Machine Learning and advanced algoritms in order to detect trash. Trash is detected automaticly not only in images but also in videos, enabling local goverments, activists and the local communities to clean up their surroundings with better efficiency than ever.
